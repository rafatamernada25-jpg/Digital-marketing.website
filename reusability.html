MODULARITY

REUSABILITY

"Don't reinvent the wheel, just build a better car."

01

DRY: Don't Repeat Yourself

Duplication is the enemy of maintenance. Every piece of knowledge must have a single, unambiguous representation within a system.

02

The Rule of Three

When you find yourself writing the same logic for the third time, it is time to extract it into a reusable component. This is the threshold of efficient engineering.

03

Modular Design

Break down complex systems into smaller, independent modules. High-quality components are pluggable—they should work in different projects with minimal changes.
